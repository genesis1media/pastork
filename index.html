Pastor K 

KMH Ministries, founded by Pastor Karey M. Heyward aka Pastor K,
has a vision and active plan to minister the Gospel of Jesus Christ  to the intent
that many would come into a loving relationship with Jesus Christ, empowering the
Body of Christ when given moments to travel and minister The Word of The Lord, and hosting tours and other events.

Welcome to the website of Pastor Karey M. Heyward, aka Pastor K. This website is a one stop shop for the various ministries and resources provided by Pastor K under the umbrella name, KMH Ministries. 

From Pastoring in Charleston SC, radio podcast host, Christian rapper, youth event hoster, traveling to minister at conferences, services, youth services, and a host of other operations, Pastor K is relentless and diligent in his pursuit to remain forward in Kingdom Advancement, Gospel preaching, soul reaching, and seeing lives change by the name & power of Jesus Christ
